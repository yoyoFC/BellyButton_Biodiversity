Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

This website was designed to collaborate with a biological research conducted by the company Improbable Beef. The main goal is to analyze and compare bacteria to synthesize protein that taste like beef. Under this hypothesis, we will analyze bacteria found in the human body, specifically in the belly button. Different people can harvest different bacteria species. This website will provide detailed information from multiple tests and participants. Each person can be identified with an ID, and each sample will provide information about the number and type of bacteria found in their belly button.
